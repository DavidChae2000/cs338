1. ifconfig
2. nslookup [hostname]
3. nslookup [ip address]
4. scp [file] [destination]
5. netstat or fuser [port]
6. nmap -sT -O [host]
7. wget "[webpage]" -O filename
8. curl [webpage]
9. For question 7, add -S. For question 8, add -v.
